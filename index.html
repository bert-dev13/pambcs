About PAMBCS

The Protected Area Management and Biodiversity Conservation System (PAMBCS) is a comprehensive digital platform designed to support the Department of Environment and Natural Resources – Cagayan Valley (DENR-02) in managing and monitoring protected areas across Region 02. This integrated system provides essential tools and resources for effective environmental governance, biodiversity conservation, and sustainable resource management.
